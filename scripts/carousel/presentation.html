The core principle

AI belongs in actuarial work as a tool — not as an expert opinion.

The failure mode is the black box: figures we accept as-is, without understanding where they came from. The intelligence performs complex tasks correctly, as defined. It informs the opinion. It never becomes the opinion.

What the governance caught

The governance found real bugs.

  • A pandas default silently dropped an undefined origin from reserve totals — a wrong number that looked valid.
  • "Unknown" was being reported as "known zero". Different lie, same class.
  • The CLI quietly aliased a stale flag — caught by re-running documented commands, not re-reading them.

The failures that live in spreadsheets for years. Surfaced in week one.
